Introduce Myself

I'm Joseph Chinedu, Frontend Developer

I love programming, blogging, & tweeting, you can call me netizen. I don't like talking about myself in the 3rd person. I'm the founder & CEO of Satelite Box, Tech blog in Nigeria. I specialize in building UX & UI specific to the business needs of my clients. I'm interested in all kinds of visual communication, but my major focus is on designing web. When I'm not coding, tweeting or blogging, you'll find me cooking.
